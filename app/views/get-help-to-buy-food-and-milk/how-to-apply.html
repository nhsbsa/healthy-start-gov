How to apply

If you’ve received an invitation letter from the NHS

You can apply online. To apply, you will need your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society account details
  • baby’s due date (if you’re pregnant)
Apply now

on the NHS website

If you’ve not received an invitation letter from the NHS, you can apply for Healthy Start vouchers instead.
