Overview

If you’re pregnant or have a child under 4, you can get help to buy healthy food and milk.

If you’re eligible, you’ll be paid money into your bank account to spend on food and milk.

You can also collect:

  • pregnancy vitamins
  • breastfeeding vitamins
  • vitamins for children aged 6 months to 5 years old

This service won't affect any of your other benefits

If you live in Scotland

You can get help from Best Start Foods instead
