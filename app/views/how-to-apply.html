How to apply

If you’ve received an invitation letter from NHS

You can apply online. To apply, you will need your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society account details

If you’ve not received an invitation letter from NHS

Apply for Healthy Start vouchers by filling in the online form and printing it out.
